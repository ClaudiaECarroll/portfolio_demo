About

Digital humanities researcher specializing in AI and Victorian literature

Background

Ph.D. in English Literature (University of Notre Dame, 2023). Currently Postdoctoral Research Fellow at TRIADS and Lecturer in the Interdisciplinary Program in the Humanities at Washington University in St. Louis.

Research focus: digital humanities, cognitive narratology, and computational approaches to nineteenth-century fiction.

Contact: claudiac@wustl.edu

Current Work

  • LLM Research: AI-generated literary text analysis with Gabi Kirilloff
  • Character Studies: Machine learning for reader cognition in realist novels
  • AI Narratology: LLM classification for narrative concepts

Recognition

  • Alan Nadel Prize, International Society for the Study of Narrative (2022)
  • TRIADS Postdoctoral Fellowship (2023-Present)
