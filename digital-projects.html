Graduate Student Collaboration

AI Art Generation Bias Analysis

Student Researcher: Sarah Chen (PhD candidate)

An investigation of bias in AI art generation systems, examining how training data and algorithmic design influence the types of artistic content these systems can produce. The project combines computational analysis with art historical expertise.

Findings have revealed significant biases toward Western art traditions and have informed discussions about more inclusive training datasets for creative AI systems.

AI Art Bias Analysis Art History Student Research
Undergraduate Research Project

Fan Fiction Network Analysis

Student Researchers: Maya Patel, Jordan Brooks (Undergraduate Honors Students)

A computational analysis of relationship networks and character development patterns in fan fiction communities. Students used network analysis and natural language processing to study how fan communities engage with and transform original media properties.

The project resulted in conference presentations and has contributed to broader discussions about transformative creativity and community-driven cultural production.
