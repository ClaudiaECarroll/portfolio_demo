Teaching

Training students in computational methods for literary analysis and digital humanities research

Teaching Philosophy

I focus on integrating computational methods with traditional literary analysis, helping students develop both technical skills and critical thinking abilities. My teaching emphasizes the intersection of digital humanities, narrative theory, and computational text analysis.
